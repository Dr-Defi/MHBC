Rewards & Points Accumulation

1. Base Monthly Rewards

Members automatically earn 12,000 loyalty points each month after the membership payment is successfully processed.

  • Monthly Points Value: $25.00
  • Annual Base Rewards: $300.00

2. Service Credit Points

When a scheduled service is not utilized, the dollar value of that service is automatically converted to bonus loyalty points and credited to your account.

Example: If you do not schedule or you cancel your monthly house cleaning (valued at $220), you will receive bonus loyalty points equivalent to that full service value (105,600 points).

How Service Credits Work

  • Services must be scheduled at least 48 hours in advance to be considered utilized.
  • If a service is not scheduled within the monthly service window, or is cancelled without proper notice, the full service value converts to loyalty points.
  • Bonus points are credited to your account within 5 business days of the missed service window.
  • There is no penalty for unused services—you simply receive the value as loyalty points instead.

Point Conversion & Redemption

Service credits convert at full dollar value to loyalty points.

  • Conversion Rate: 480 points equals $1.00
  • Annual Potential: Members typically accumulate approximately $600 in loyalty points annually ($300 base + $300 avg. credits).
  • Redemption: Points can be redeemed for additional services, service upgrades, or special requests not included in your standard membership package.
  • Expiration: Points do not expire as long as membership remains active.
  • Non-Transferable: Points are non-transferable and have no cash value.

Cancellation Policy

  • Service cancellations must be made at least 48 hours in advance to receive service credit points.
  • Cancellations made with less than 48 hours notice will not receive service credit points.
  • Emergency cancellations due to weather or facility issues will be rescheduled without penalty.
  • Membership may be cancelled with 30 days written notice. Unused points are forfeited upon membership cancellation.
